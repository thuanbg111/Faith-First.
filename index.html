FAITH FIRST

We are a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our specific purpose is to promote racial reconciliation among faith communities through education, dialogue, and service.

Learn more See programs

What We Do

Practical work that brings people of faith together.

  • Dialogue & learning circles on reconciliation and justice
  • Community service projects with interfaith partners
  • Training for leaders on inclusive practices

Education & Dialogue

Workshops and circles that build empathy and shared understanding.

Service & Outreach

Joint projects meeting needs while modeling unity in action.

Leadership Training

Practical tools for clergy and volunteers to lead reconciliatory communities.
