About Faith First

Faith First is organized under California’s Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation Law and operates for charitable purposes as a recognized 501(c)(3) with EIN 85-4036862.

Specific purpose: promote racial reconciliation among faith communities.

  • No substantial part of our activities involves lobbying or political campaigning.
  • Assets are irrevocably dedicated to charitable purposes.
  • Upon dissolution, remaining assets go to a similar 501(c)(3).
